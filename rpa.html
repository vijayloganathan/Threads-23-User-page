Robotic Process Automation

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) is a software technology that automates repetitive, rule-based tasks by emulating human interactions with digital systems. RPA software robots can be programmed to interact with various software applications, websites, and systems to perform tasks such as data entry, data extraction, form filling, and other repetitive tasks that would otherwise require human intervention.
