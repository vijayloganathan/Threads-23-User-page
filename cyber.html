Cyber Security

A cybersecurity workshop is a training program designed to educate individuals, organizations, or groups about cybersecurity practices, threats, and best practices. The workshop typically covers a wide range of topics related to cybersecurity. A cybersecurity workshop may include hands-on training, simulations, and case studies to help participants apply the knowledge gained in the workshop

24/02/2023, 10AM - 4PM

Dennis Ritchie Lab

DESCRIPTION

This workshop aims to offer students a spark and a fundamental knowledge about principles of cybersecurity by instructing them about network traffic analysis, exploitation of various operating systems, network hacking, browser hacking, binary exploitation, various tools in Kali Linux, and practical experience on some malwares which gives a lot more knowledge.
